About Us

Our Story

IronCloudMC was founded in 2019 by HASAN as a private world for a close group of friends. What started as a simple idea - a place to create and have fun together - has grown into one of the most welcoming Minecraft communities.

The name reflects our spirit: Iron for strength and permanence, and Cloud for our limitless creativity. From our original spawn town to the sprawling kingdoms of today, every player has left their mark on our server's history.

Server History

2019: IRON CLOUD MC was born as a private server for friends

2020: Opened to the public with custom plugins and features

2021: Expanded to support both Java and Bedrock editions

2022: Major updates with new game modes and features

2023: Continued growth with an active community of thousands

Our Mission

At IRON CLOUD MC, we believe that Minecraft is more than just a game - it's a platform for creativity, friendship, and community building. Our mission is to provide a safe, enjoyable environment where players of all ages can explore, build, and create amazing memories together.

We're committed to regular updates, fair gameplay, and maintaining the friendly atmosphere that has defined our server since the beginning.

What Makes Us Different

Community First: We listen to our players and implement features based on community feedback.

Regular Updates: Our server is constantly evolving with new content and improvements.

Cross-Platform: We support both Java and Bedrock editions, bringing players together.

Dedicated Staff: Our team works tirelessly to ensure a great experience for everyone.

Fair Play: We maintain strict rules against cheating and ensure fair gameplay for all.
