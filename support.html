Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my purchase?

All purchases are delivered instantly after payment confirmation. If you don't receive your items within 5 minutes, please contact support.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Bkash and Nagad for Bangladesh users. International payment methods are coming soon.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are generally not provided for digital purchases. However, if you didn't receive your items due to a technical error, we'll make sure you get what you paid for.

Do you support both Java and Bedrock?

Yes! Our server supports both Minecraft Java Edition and Bedrock Edition players.

What versions of Minecraft do you support?

We support versions from 1.8 to the latest version (1.21.8).
